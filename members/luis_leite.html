Bio
Luis Leite (aka Grifu) is a multidisciplinary artist , developer and researcher with particular focus on digital animation and interactive media. Works in ESMAD as a teacher, assuming the direction of the Multimedia Department and the direction of the Master’s in Interactive Media and Systems. His motivation is to create innovative solutions for impossible challenges and to facilitate the use of technologies as creative tools. Holds a PhD in Digital Media (Cum Laude) and a MA in Multimedia, both from University of Porto in the fields of digital puppetry and performance animation, and a bachelor in Multimedia at ISMAI. His professional profile begun in the early 1990’s as a computer programmer, and gradually progressed to the audiovisual, and the multimedia fields, working in studios such as Miragem and Animago. Participated in solo and group exhibitions and collaborated in productions such as “Major Alvega” (Live Action plus animation TV Series - Emmy award selection), “História de um Caramelo” (Short animated film), “Peregrinação” (Live Cinema), “Frágil” (Puppet play), “Solitária” (Performance).
