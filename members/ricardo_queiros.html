Bio
Ricardo Queirós holds a PhD on Computer Science and is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Media Arts and Design School of the Polytechnic Institute of Porto where he teaches mainly Web and Mobile programming. He is also a researcher in the field of e-learning interoperability and programming languages learning at the Center for Research in Advanced Computing Systems (CRACS) research group of INESC TEC Porto. He is also the Director of uniMAD a Research Unit of Media Arts and Design. He is currently working in the design and implementation of gamified learning environments to foster the teaching-learning process. He is also the author of 10 books regarding Web/Mobile development and more than 100 scientific publications focused on computer science education, gamification and interoperability in e-learrning systems.
