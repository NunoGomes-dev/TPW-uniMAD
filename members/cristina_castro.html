Bio
Cristina Castro (Porto, 1974) lives and works in Porto. Doctoral student in Contemporary Art at the College of Arts of the University of Coimbra, her research studies the visual possibilities of perception of the invisible occurrences in pictorial landscape, depicting a game of presence and absence. Lecturer of drawing and contemporary art at the School of Media Arts and Design, Polytechnic Institute of Porto (ESMAD), where she coordinates the Foundation course. She holds a Master degree in Museology and Cultural Heritage by the Faculty of Arts of the University of Coimbra and a degree in Fine Arts Painting by the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto (FBAUP). She taught mosaic and stained glass at FBAUP, scenography at RTP média parque, drawing at the Government College of Art and Craft, Calcutta and at the School of Education of the Polytechnic Institute of Bragança. She has created the museum programme for Casa Ateliê Oliveira Ferreira, Miramar, Vila Nova de Gaia City Council. She participates in solo and group exhibitions since 1995.
