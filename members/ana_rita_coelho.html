Bio
Rita Coelho, PhD in Art and Design (Faculty of Arts UP, 2014) with the theme Brand Semiotics: contributions for the construction of a syntax and visual rethoric in graphic design practice, and Master in Multimedia Art (Faculty of Arts UP, 2008), is an Adjunct Professor in School of Media Arts and Design, Porto Polytechnic, where she was coordinator of the Design Master and teaches in the field of Communication Design. Researches in her field of expertise: visual identity design, communication design and drawing; received a doctoral scholarship by FCT and contributes to the research centers of ID+ and uniMAD. Lectured and taught workshops in ESMAD; FBAUP in the MA of Graphic Design and Editorial Projects; in Camberwell University of the Arts London; in FBAUL in the PhD in Design; in IPCA; in ESE and ESMAE of Porto Polytechnic. She has participated in several national and international conferences, such as in Central Saint Martins College of Arts and Design and published scientific papers on her field. Has received awards such as the graphic identity for the magazine of Parks and Wildlife, the Award Ant√≥nio de Almeida Foundation and the visual identity for the Couto Foundation; has worked on identity and communication design for clients such as WESC, Agros, Municipality of Santa Maria da Feira and University of Porto, among others. She graduated in Communication Design (Graphic Art) in Faculty of Arts UP, attended the Architecture course at FAUP and studied Design at Willem de Kooning Academie - Hogeschool Rotterdam.
