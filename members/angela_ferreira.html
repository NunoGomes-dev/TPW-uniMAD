Bio
Artist, curator and researcher in the field of Contemporary Photography and Culture. PhD in Visual Communication from the University of Minho, having a scholarship from the Foundation for Science and Technology; Master in Photography, from the Utrecht School of Arts-Netherlands and a Law Degree from University of Minho. She holds a postdoctoral at School of Fine Arts at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, developing studies on contemporary visual practices that problematize the hybrid forms of photography, in intersection with other languages, such as painting, cinema and literature. Member of the research group NANO Organisms of New Arts at the School of Fine Arts at UFRJ. (NanoLab), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Adjunct Guest Teacher at Polytechnic Institute of Porto (School of Media Arts and Design) where she coordinated the Summer School / International Summer School dedicated to Visual Storytelling and photobooks. She lectures contemporary photography throughout Europe and Latin American countries, with emphasis on Brazil and works as a curator of national and international exhibitions.

Research interests

  • Contemporary photography
  • Visual Culture and Post-Photography
  • Photobooks and vernacular narratives
